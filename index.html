My Awards

Over the past few years, I have been actively participating in various school, state, national and international competitions ranging from Science and Mathematics to Martial Arts and Sports. Being able to participate in these competitions is not just about the achievements, but the passion and wonderful experiences

BIEA Repack to Restore Our World STEM COMPETITION

International: United Kingdom (2021)

Organised by the British International Education Association (BIEA), we need to come up with 4 prototypes to substitute the use of plastics packaging besides conducting 8 experiments to test the prototypes. We were fizzing with excitement to announce that our team "TEAM CURIOUS" has become the first Malaysian team to win the Grand Prize!

International Science Competition (ISC)
